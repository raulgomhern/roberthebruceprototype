Discover the relevance of Robert the Bruce for the history of Scotland, his last wish and the archaeological findings related to his burial.

Throughout this journey, explore the anatomical analyses of his body and skull from nineteenth-century phrenological studies to the latest scientific research and modern facial reconstructions.
